Research Focus

◈

Legged Locomotion

Developing robust control frameworks for bipedal and quadrupedal robots navigating complex, unstructured terrain.

△

Multi-Agent Systems

Scalable coordination algorithms that enable emergent cooperative behavior in large swarms of autonomous agents.

◯

Biomechanics & Neuroscience

Uncovering neural and mechanical principles of animal locomotion to inspire bio-inspired robotic design.

◆

Learning & Adaptation

Reinforcement learning and sim-to-real transfer for adaptive motor control in uncertain environments.
