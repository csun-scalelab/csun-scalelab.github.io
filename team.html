Principal Investigator
LC

Dr. Lena Chen

Associate Professor — Thomas Lord Dept. of Computer Science, USC

Dr. Chen leads the SCALE Lab with a focus on the intersection of control theory, machine learning, and biomechanics. Prior to joining USC, she was a postdoctoral researcher at Carnegie Mellon University's Robotics Institute. Her work has been recognized with the NSF CAREER Award (2025) and the IEEE RAS Early Career Award (2023).
