Principal Investigator

Dr. Sina Heydari

Assistant Professor — Department of Mechanical Engineering, CSUN

Dr. Heydari leads the SCALE Lab at California State University, Northridge. He completed his PhD at USC's Bio-inspired Motion Lab under Professor Eva Kanso, and previously taught undergraduate and graduate courses at Santa Clara University. His research combines physics-based models with data-driven approaches — specifically Reinforcement Learning — to study and control bio-inspired dynamical systems.
